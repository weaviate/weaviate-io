<!-- Provide context for this course, in addition to the concrete learning goals and outcomes. Why would someone want to do this unit? -->

Weaviate integrates with a variety of vectorizer models, each designed to create vector representations, or embeddings, of data.

These vectorizers differ in capabilities, use cases, resource demands, and costs, among other factors. Selecting the most appropriate vectorizer for your specific needs is crucial.

In this unit, you will explore the range of vectorizers available in Weaviate and learn how to choose the best-suited vectorizer for your application.


### <i class="fa-solid fa-clipboard-list-check"></i> Prerequisites

- A foundational understanding of vector representations.
- Familiarity with Weaviate's vector search capabilities.
- Intermediate proficiency in Python programming.
