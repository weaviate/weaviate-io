Weaviate offers three distinct approaches to search: vector, keyword, and hybrid. Each method has its unique strengths and applicabilities, making the selection critical to the success of your search-related tasks.

In this section, we delve into the intricacies of these search types to equip you with the knowledge to discern when and why to employ each of these search methodologies.

We will explore how the choice of search type impacts not only the quality of the search results but also the overall performance of the search operation.
