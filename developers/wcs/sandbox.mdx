Weaviate Cloud Services (WCS) provides an interactive console that you can use to create and manage WCS clusters. Follow these steps to create a new WCS account.

## Create a new user account

1. Go the [WCS homepage](https://console.weaviate.cloud).
1. Click, "Register here".

![Create an account](./img/wcs-landing-page-register.jpg)

3. Provide an email address and password.
1. Click `Create an account`.
1. Check your email account for a verification email.
1. Click the link to verify your email address.

After you verify your email, you can log into the WCS Console.

## Configure billing information

Sandbox instances are free, short term instances that expire after 14 days. If you are using a Sandbox instance to try Weaviate, you don't have to set up billing.

Weaviate offers three types of paid hosting:

- Serverless
- Enterprise Dedicated
- Bring Your Own Cloud

([Contact our sales team](https://weaviate.io/pricing#contact-sales) to discuss which solution is right for you.)

To configure billing, follow these steps. 

1. Click the cogwheel icon at the left hand side of the console.
1. Scroll down to `Billing Information`.
1. Fill out the form and click `Save`.
1. Enter your credit card information on the next screen.
