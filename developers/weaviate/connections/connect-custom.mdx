The [Python Client v4](/developers/weaviate/client-libraries/python) and the [TypeScript Client v3](/developers/weaviate/client-libraries/typescript/typescript-v3) provide helper methods for common connection types. They also provide custom methods for when you need additional connection configuration.

If you are using one of the other clients, the standard connection methods are configurable for all connections.
