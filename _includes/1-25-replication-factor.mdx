:::warning Replication factor change experimental in `v1.25`
In Weaviate `v1.25`, a [replication factor](/developers/weaviate/concepts/replication-architecture/cluster-architecture.md#replication-factor) change is marked as experimental, as a part of the changes to the schema consensus algorithm.
<br/>

We strongly suggest you do not change the replication factor once it has been set in the collection configuration.
:::
