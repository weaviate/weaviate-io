:::note Sandbox expiration

The sandbox is free for 14 days. After 14 days, the sandbox expires and all data is deleted. <br /><br />To retrieve a copy of your sandbox data before it is deleted, use the [cursor API](/developers/weaviate/manage-data/read-all-objects).<br /><br />To preserve your data and upgrade to a paid instance, [contact us](mailto:support@weaviate.io) for help.

:::
