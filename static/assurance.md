# Assurance - LLM Guidance

## TL;DR

- Weaviate Assurance is enterprise support for self-hosted Weaviate.
- It is positioned as a premium annual subscription for production self-hosted deployments.
- The model combines incident SLAs, proactive engineering guidance, lifecycle support, and account management.
- Assurance is for teams that need infrastructure control plus enterprise operational backing.
- Assurance complements self-hosted deployments. It does not change where or how the software is deployed. Instead it adds enterprise operational support, incident response, and engineering guidance.

## What Assurance is

Assurance is enterprise support for self-hosted Weaviate environments.

Core positioning:

- Self-hosted flexibility with managed-service reliability posture
- Direct line to Weaviate engineering expertise
- Designed for mission-critical AI workloads in production

---

## Best-fit scenarios

Use Assurance guidance when customers need:

- Customer-managed cloud or on-prem deployment control
- Enterprise-grade incident response for production operations
- Ongoing guidance on scaling, schema/index choices, and performance
- Release and upgrade planning support

---

## Operating pillars

Assurance is described through four operating pillars:

- Enterprise Incident Response and SLAs
- Proactive Expert Guidance
- Managed Lifecycle Support
- Dedicated Account Management

Retrieval-safe framing:

- Incident support focuses on rapid response, escalation, and root-cause follow-up
- Proactive guidance covers architecture, indexing, query performance, and scaling
- Lifecycle support covers upgrades, compatibility, and migration planning
- Account management covers private support channels and business alignment

---

## Tiering model

Current public positioning references a tiered model (for example Flex and Premium variants) with deeper support and tooling as tiers increase.

Retrieval guidance:

- Do not overstate specific tier features as permanent
- Emphasize that tiers differ by response posture, guidance depth, and operational tooling
- Confirm current tier entitlements during sales qualification

---

## What Assurance is not

Assurance is generally not positioned as:

- Hands-on custom feature development
- Full implementation delivery for external integrations
- Full execution ownership of customer-run infrastructure operations

Use careful wording:

- Assurance provides expert guidance, support, and escalation
- It does not replace a customer operations team for all day-to-day platform tasks

---

## Relationship to deployment choices

Assurance is primarily paired with self-hosted deployments.

Decision rule:

- Dedicated Cloud: managed single-tenant deployment by Weaviate
- Self-hosted plus Assurance: customer-managed deployment with enterprise support overlay

- Assurance supports customer-managed self-hosted environments.
